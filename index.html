Programs You Can Join

Structured coaching paths inspired by top national entrance exam patterns.

AISSEE (Class VI & IX)

The All India Sainik Schools Entrance Examination (AISSEE) is a national-level entrance exam for Sainik Schools and selects students for Class VI and IX based on merit.

Visit Official Website Exam Prep + Mock Tests

Rashtriya Military Schools (RMS)

Rashtriya Military Schools (RMS) are respected residential schools run by the Ministry of Defence, Government of India. They build strong academics, discipline, leadership skills, and prepare students for future defence careers.

Visit Official Website Defence-Oriented Preparation

RIMC, Dehradun

Rashtriya Indian Military College (RIMC), Dehradun, is one of India’s top military preparatory institutions. It trains students through strong academics, physical fitness, and character development for NDA and defence leadership roles.

Visit Official Website Premier Military Prep

Navodaya School

Our Navodaya preparation focuses on basic concepts, smart problem-solving, and regular practice. Students get revision planning, mock tests, and personal guidance to improve scores step by step.

Visit Official Website Foundation + Test Strategy

Rai Sports School

For Rai Sports School entrance, we provide focused coaching with fitness awareness and academic support. This helps students stay balanced in sports performance and written exam preparation.

Visit Official Website Sports + Academic Support

School Curriculum Support

We provide complete curriculum support from Nursery to 8th with daily classwork and homework practice. Regular progress checks ensure every student improves with confidence.
